### 🚀 About Me
I am a **Computer Science & Engineering** student at **PES University**, deeply passionate about bridging the gap between intelligent software and physical systems. My work spans **Robotics & Computer Vision**, **Artificial Intelligence (Machine Learning & GenAI)**, and modern **Full-Stack Web Development**.

Whether I'm developing reinforcement learning models for robotic surgery, designing adaptive tutoring systems using Explainable AI, or building high-performance TypeScript applications, I love turning complex logic into smooth, working solutions.

- 🎓 **Education:** Pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering at PES University (EC Campus).
- 🤖 **Current Obsession:** Reinforcement learning for robotic motion planning and autonomous drone mapping.
- ⚙️ **Learning Journey:** Currently diving deep into **Rust**, mastering the **MEAN Stack**, and exploring advanced **Machine Learning** algorithms.
- 🤝 **Collaboration:** I'm always looking to collaborate on impactful Open Source projects, robotics workspaces, or innovative web applications.
